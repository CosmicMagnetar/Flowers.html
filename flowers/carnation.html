About the Carnation

Symbolism and Uses

Carnations are one of the most popular cut flowers, used frequently in flower arrangements, corsages, and boutonnieres. Different colors carry different meanings: red carnations symbolize love and admiration, white represents pure love and good luck, and pink carnations are associated with a mother's undying love, making them a popular Mother's Day gift.
